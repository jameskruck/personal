About Me

My approach is grounded in the belief that the best teaching emerges from collaborative environments where instructors, developers, and learners contribute to shared growth. Whether I'm designing professional development workshops, implementing learning management systems, or leading curriculum initiatives, my focus is on creating meaningful, data-driven learning experiences that foster engagement and measurable outcomes.

With experience in both higher education and the private sector, I've led the development of competency-based programs while ensuring full accessibility compliance. I'm passionate about creating inclusive, engaging learning experiences that leverage the best of technology while remaining firmly grounded in sound pedagogical principles.

Currently pursuing PMP certification and having recently completed Business Analytics training, I'm committed to continuous professional growth and bringing current best practices to every project.
