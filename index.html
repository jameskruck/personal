Welcome

Hi, I'm James! As a learning technology and curriculum specialist, I've spent over 15 years developing expertise in digital learning design, evidence-based curriculum development, and universal design for accessibility. My work spans both higher education and the private sector, where I've led the development of over 130 competency-based online and blended programs while ensuring full accessibility compliance.

Whether I'm designing professional development workshops, implementing learning management systems, or leading curriculum initiatives, my focus is on creating meaningful, data-driven learning experiences. I believe in building programs that not only meet rigorous academic standards but also create genuine engagement and measurable outcomes for all learners.

Currently pursuing PMP certification and recently completed Business Analytics training, I'm committed to continuous professional growth and bringing current best practices to every project.
