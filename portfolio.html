Adult and professional learning at scale

Where the practice was built

Before Ivey, I led curriculum for a national occupational health and safety provider, designing learning for a workforce of adult professionals. Over nine years I built more than 130 competency-based learning experiences across online, blended, and in-person formats, established the quality assurance and learning-analytics processes behind them, and brought all digital content into AODA and WCAG 2.1 compliance.

That work is the foundation under the AI and learning-design projects above. The instinct to start from a real learner need, design for adults who are busy and self-directed, and finish with something usable comes directly from it.
