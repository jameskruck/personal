Teaching Philosophy

My teaching philosophy is built on engagement, accessibility, and active learning. I create inclusive environments by leveraging retrieval practice, scaffolded assessments, and interactive discussions. My approach is informed by universal design principles to ensure equitable learning experiences for all students.

Teaching Experience

  • Lecturer, University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign – Readings in Latin Literature
  • Visiting Faculty, Quest University – Fate and Virtue
  • Instructor, University of Western Ontario – Introduction to Latin
  • Teaching Assistant, University of Western Ontario – Classical Civilization

Workshops

I develop and facilitate educator workshops focused on active learning and accessibility.

  • UDL Plus One Workshop: Introduces incremental applications of Universal Design for Learning.
  • Mastering Effective Questioning Techniques: Teaches strategies to enhance student engagement through questioning.
  • Modeling and Providing Examples: Focuses on using structured modeling to clarify complex concepts.
