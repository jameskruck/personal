About Me

I'm an educational developer passionate about creating inclusive, engaging learning experiences that leverage the best of technology while remaining firmly grounded in sound pedagogical principles.

With experience in both humanities and technical disciplines, I bridge the gap between traditional academic approaches and innovative digital environments. My work focuses on helping faculty implement evidence-based teaching strategies that support diverse learners.

I believe that thoughtful educational design can transform learning experiences, making them more accessible, engaging, and effective for all students. My approach integrates Universal Design for Learning principles with cutting-edge digital tools to create learning environments that adapt to student needs rather than requiring students to adapt to rigid structures.
